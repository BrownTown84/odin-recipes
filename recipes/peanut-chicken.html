Peanut Chicken

Description

Made with a few simple ingredients, this simple dish is sweet and spicy with a delicious peanut curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 LB Chicken Breast, cubed to bite sized pieces
  • 1/2 Onion, Finely Diced
  • 1/2 Bell Pepper, Diced
  • 1 Minced Garlic Clove
  • 1 Tsp Ground Ginger
  • 2 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Peanut Butter
  • 1 Tbsp Chilli Crisps
  • 1 Tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1/4 Cup Coconut Milk
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • (Optionally) Rice

Recipe

  1. Heat pan over Medium-High heat, pour enough olive oil to cover bottom of pan
  2. Add onions, bell peppers, garlic, and ginger to pan, cook until soft
  3. Once mixture is soft, add chicken. Cook until 165 degrees fahrenheit
  4. Add brown sugar, soy sauce, peanut butter, chilli crisps, cayenne pepper, and splash of coconut milk to pan. Reduce until thickened.
  5. Serve immediately, optionally with rice.
